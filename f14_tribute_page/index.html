LucasArts Adventure Games

Era of masterpiece point and click adventures

From the late 1980s to the early 2000s, LucasArts was well known for their point-and-click graphic adventure games, nearly all of which got high scoring reviews at the time of their release:

  • 1986

    Labyrinth

  • 1987

    Maniac Mansion

  • 1988

    Zak McKracken and the Alien Mindbenders

  • 1989

    Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade

  • 1990

    Loom

  • 1990

    The Secret of Monkey Island

  • 1991

    Monkey Island 2

  • 1992

    Indiana Jones and the Fate of Atlantis

  • 1993

    Day of the Tentacle

  • 1993

    Sam & Max Hit the Road

  • 1995

    Full Throttle

  • 1995

    The Dig

  • 1997

    The Curse of Monkey Island

  • 1998

    Grim Fandango

  • 2000

    Escape from Monkey Island

The player should be entertained, focusing on story and exploration, instead of being excessively punished for mistakes or frustrated by trial and error.
-- LucasArts' game design philosophy
