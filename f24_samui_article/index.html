Best places to see on Koh Samui

Ko Samui (or Koh Samui), often locally shortened to Samui, is an island off the east coast of Thailand. Geographically in the Chumphon Archipelago, it is part of Surat Thani Province. Ko Samui, with an area of 228.7 square kilometres (88.3 sq mi), is Thailand's second largest island after Phuket. In 2018, it was visited by 2.7 million tourists.

Snorkeling introduction

There will be nothing to compare with first sight of underwater world full of amazing beautiful life. Coral reefs near Silver Beach and Coral Cove area are one of the best around. When it would be not enough, there is an island of Ko Tao in a deeper waters to go dive into.

Bicycling around the island

Much convenient circle road around whole island is about 60km, for average of 3 hours riding. With proper planned trip making several stops and turns-in, it's possible to see the most of important spots on the way.
